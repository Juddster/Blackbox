# Blackbox Future Features

This document is the central backlog for ideas that are intentionally outside v1 but worth preserving.

The goal is to keep these ideas visible without letting them blur the v1 scope.

## Principles

- This is not a priority-ordered roadmap yet.
- Inclusion here does not mean commitment.
- Items here should be framed briefly and clearly enough to revisit later.

## Future Feature List

## 1. Sleep As A Passive Segment Type

Allow sleep to appear as another semantic segment type, likely driven more by watch and Health data than by phone motion.

## 2. Broader Health History

Expand beyond heart rate to long-term health and body metrics such as:
- weight
- blood pressure
- body measurements
- VO2 max
- BMI
- respiratory metrics
- blood oxygen
- other HealthKit-compatible metrics

## 3. Deeper Activity Taxonomy

Support narrower labels beyond the broad v1 classes, such as:
- hiking as a stronger first-class automatic label
- driving
- motorcycle
- train
- bus
- public transportation
- swimming
- rowing
- sailing
- speed boat
- stair climbing

## 4. Richer Apple Watch Experience

Move beyond current-activity UI and basic standalone usefulness toward a more complete watch-side Blackbox experience.

## 5. Web UI

Provide browser-based access to Blackbox history, maps, collections, and exports.

## 6. Android App

Extend the Blackbox model and product to Android devices.

## 7. Garmin And Third-Party Device Integrations

Support additional wearable and fitness-device sources beyond Apple platforms.

## 8. Richer Health And Trend Views

Add longitudinal views, comparisons, and trend analysis across health and activity data.

## 9. Live Sharing

Allow the user to create a revocable live share link that lets others follow an ongoing activity or trip on a map.

Desired behavior:
- share via slugged link
- live updates while sharing is active
- ability to stop updating while preserving what was already shared
- ability to revoke access entirely

## 10. Trip Reports

Allow a live share or completed activity/collection to become a richer narrative record with:
- comments
- photos
- location-linked updates
- durable post-trip viewing

This could evolve into a hybrid of collection, export, and live-sharing concepts.

## 11. Destination-Specific Export Templates

Add richer export variants tailored to specific destinations such as:
- Strava-style exports
- Instagram/TikTok-oriented exports
- richer day/story exports

## 12. Public Or Multi-User Cloud Readiness

Extend the storage and cloud model toward true multi-user support while preserving tenant separation and security.

## Notes

When a future feature starts becoming concrete, it should usually get its own focused design doc rather than expanding this file too much.
